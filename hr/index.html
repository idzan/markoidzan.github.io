O meni

Pozdrav, moje ime je Marko. Rođen sam u rujnu 1993. godine. Trenutno sam student izvanrednog preddiplomskog studija računarstva. Uz studiranje, povremeno se bavim i YouTube-om i izradom sadržaja za tu platformu. Uza sve to bavim se i fotografijom te izradom web stranica. Isto tako, veoma dobro poznajem društvene mreže tako da sam za Social Media Managment vrlo dobar. Moje vjersko opredjeljenje je Kršćanin - Rimokatolik.

Za obrazovanje mogu reći da je odluka za IT bila teška i zeznuta. Sve je počelo u Osnovnoj školi Žitnjak, Zagreb. Od 5.-og razreda pa nadalje sam se vidio u IT branši. Sve je počelo sa izradom jednostavne web stranice koristeći WYSIYWG Web Buildera u 6.-om razredu osnovne. Kod odabira srednje škole odlučio asm ići u Tehničku školu Ruđera Boškovića, Zagreb. U srednjoj sam naučio puno toga o arhitekturi PC računala, održavanju i umrežavanju različitih računalnih sustava te nešto i o administraciji sustava. Ipak, najbolje doba je doba srednje. Odluka za fakultet je bila relativno lagana. Jedan od izbora je bio i Tehničko veleučilište u Zagrebu. Najteži odabir je bio na koji odjel upisati. Ja sam želio ići na Informatički odjel ili Odjel računarstva, ali slijedom okolnosti na stranicama postani-student.hr sam ispao sa lista. Zbog toga sam upisao Elektrotehnički odjel Tehničkog veleučilišta. Na Elektrotehničkom odjelu sam odabrao smjer Komunikacijska i računalna tehnika. Zbog određenih okolnosti sam nakon 4 godine redovitog studija na Elektrotehničkom odjelu morao prestati tamo studirati. Od opcija koje su ostale za nastavak studija odabrao sam Izvanredni preddiplomski studij računarstva, gdje trenutno studiram.

Radno iskustvo i vještine

Vezano uz radno iskustvo, mogu reći da sam bio nezaposlen student tijekom vremena redovnog studija. Tijekom tog doba sam imao manje IT poslove za prijatelje i rodbinu. Kako bi me ISK zvao, bio sam "Mali 'o tete". Za vrijeme studija sam se počeo više baviti sa izradom YouTube video sadržaja te mi je to postao "posao". Tijekom posljednje 4 godine je to postalo više kao "posao" nego hobi kao što je to bilo prije.

Skills can be described like this - most of them are learned from self paced learning schedule. Those skills also can be sorted in two major groups - programming with informatics and audio-video editing skills. Most of programming skills I learned within high school and college. In that time I learned to program in ANSI C, Java, C++ and LabView. Also in that time I learned lot about creting Small Office-Home Office Networking, Administration and basics of 3D modeling. In free time I learned about HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Content Managment Systems like WordPress and Joomla, video editing and graphics design with basic and professional tools. For most of mine works I use free and open-source applications, but for some operations I had to learn to work in professional and paid software. That software which I learned are: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Sony Vegas Pro and Techsmith Camtasia Studio.

Some Works That I Done

Website of The parish of St. Vitus, Zagreb-Petrusevec

Website of The parish of St. Vitus (Croatian: Župa sv. Vida, Zagreb-Petruševec) was originaly created in 2008. In early days of website it was powered on pure HTML and some JavaScript. I got on administration place in April of 2010. First major change was in 2012, when was website switched to WordPress. In next 3 years there was few major design changes, and lot of small changes to website. Latest design use WordPress Theme Twenty Thirteen as base for other changes.

Visit website at: www.zupa-petrusevec.hr

Website and WebShop of Maskice.hr , Zagreb

Website and WebShop of Maskice.hr is created in November of 2014. It uses WordPress as CMS and WooCommerce as e-commerce platform. Original design used iTheme2 for website theme. In early March of 2016 there was make a major change to website. That change include a brand new theme which was bought from ThemeForest. Theme that is now on website is Flatsome by UX-Themes. Website have implemented Live Chat which is powered by tawk.to.

Visit website at: maskice.hr

"Moj TVZ for Android™" Application and WebSite

"Moj TVZ za Android™" (Croatian: Moj TVZ for Android™) is the unofficial app for all students of the Polytechnic of Zagreb. The application was created as a worthy alternative to the mobile version of the portal moj.tvz.hr. The application have upgraded certain responsive elements of the interface of the portal and it is optimized for less consumption of network bandwidth. Website for this project is HTML5 based website which use Bootstrap v4 Alpha Framework. It provides a lot of details about application, from which most is technical.

Visit website at: https://midzan21.tk/MojTVZzaAndroidOfficial

Download application from: Amazon App Store
